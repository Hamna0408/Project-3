Notes + -

Top Notes: Pineapple, Bergamot, Black Currant and Apple

Middle Notes: Birch, Patchouli, Moroccan Jasmine and Rose

Base Notes: Musk, Oak Moss, Ambergris and Vanilla

What it is + -

Introducing Haider, a captivating men's fragrance that mirrors the grandeur of its name, made with warm woody notes and enchanting aroma.

Saeed Ghani’s magnificent fragrance Haider opens with a vibrant blend of pineapple, bergamot, black currant, and apple, creating a glamorous introduction that immediately commands attention. In the keynotes, there’s a mix of birch, patchouli, Moroccan jasmine, and rose weaved together for the essence of smoky woods and alluring florals. Finally, the base notes of musk, oakmoss, ambergris, and vanilla provide a solid foundation, leaving a magnetic, long-lasting trail that embodies the perfect balance of strength and sensuality.

Haider is the best choice to express the modern man's charisma and timeless charm.
